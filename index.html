About:

Hello, I'm Pabel Hernandez. To give you a brief overview, I'm a CNC machinery programmer who decided to level up and make the leap into computer programming.


I chose to study Industrial Engineering because the versatility and the ability to enhance any process were skills I wanted to integrate, not only into my work but also into my daily life. While studying engineering, I realized how well I could grasp computer-related concepts. This led me to independently learn programming, which I enjoyed so much that I enrolled in the Software Engineering career.
